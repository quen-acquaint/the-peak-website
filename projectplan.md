## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

### 1. Project Description

The Peak Hong Kong Cafe website is a premium, single-page marketing website designed to showcase Sydney's most authentic Cha Chaan Teng (Hong Kong-style tea house). The website serves as the primary digital presence for the cafe, functioning as an educational platform that introduces customers to the rich cultural heritage of Cha Chaan Teng while converting visitors into paying, in-person customers.

**Target Audience:**
- Hong Kong expats seeking authentic taste of home (Primary)
- Curious foodies interested in authentic Asian cuisine (Secondary)
- Sydney locals looking for unique dining experiences (Secondary)
- Cultural enthusiasts wanting to learn about Hong Kong heritage (Tertiary)

**Core Value Proposition:**
"Sydney's most authentic Cha Chaan Teng experience - where traditional Hong Kong culinary heritage meets fresh, quality ingredients in a premium setting."

---

### 2. Project Intention & Goals

#### Primary Intention
Convert website visitors into physical cafe visitors by:
1. **Educating** them about Cha Chaan Teng culture and heritage
2. **Inspiring** curiosity and emotional connection through storytelling
3. **Demonstrating** authenticity through menu showcases and brand narrative
4. **Facilitating** easy discovery of location and contact information

#### Success Metrics
- Increase foot traffic to physical cafe location
- Drive phone calls for reservations/inquiries
- Increase social media follows and engagement
- Build brand recognition in Sydney's dining scene
- Generate word-of-mouth marketing through cultural education

#### Secondary Goals
- Establish The Peak as the authority on authentic Cha Chaan Teng in Sydney
- Create shareable content that spreads awareness organically
- Build emotional connection with Hong Kong culture
- Differentiate from competitors through premium positioning

### 3. Current Website Structure

#### Implemented Sections (Completed ✓)

1. **Navigation Bar**
   - Fixed position with blur backdrop
   - Logo placement
   - Menu links (Our Story, Heritage, Menu, Visit Us)
   - Mobile-responsive hamburger menu

2. **Hero Section**
   - Full-screen immersive introduction
   - Value proposition headline
   - Dual CTAs: "Explore Our Menu" + "Find Us"
   - Scroll indicator for engagement

3. **Our Story Section**
   - Brand narrative and authenticity story
   - Emphasis on tradition, fresh ingredients, Hong Kong-trained chefs
   - Image placeholder for interior/team photos
   - Emotional quote highlighting cultural preservation

4. **Heritage Section**
   - Educational timeline (1950s → 1960s-80s → Today)
   - Historical context of Cha Chaan Teng culture
   - Three feature cards: Traditional Techniques, Fresh Ingredients, Authentic Atmosphere
   - Positions cafe as cultural custodians

5. **Menu Section**
   - Six signature dishes with Chinese names and descriptions:
     - Polo Bun (菠蘿包)
     - Silk Stocking Milk Tea (絲襪奶茶)
     - HK French Toast (西多士)
     - Baked Pork Chop Rice (焗豬扒飯)
     - Soup Macaroni (湯通粉)
     - Yuanyang (鴛鴦)
   - Badge system (Customer Favorite, Signature, Must Try, etc.)
   - Strong CTA to visit in person

6. **Visit Section**
   - Location details (placeholder)
   - Operating hours
   - Contact information (placeholder)
   - Direct action CTAs: "Call Now" + "Get Directions"
   - Google Maps integration placeholder

7. **Footer**
   - Branding reinforcement
   - Quick navigation links
   - Social media links
   - Copyright information

#### Technical Implementation
- **HTML5**: Semantic, SEO-optimized structure
- **CSS3**: Custom styling with CSS variables for brand consistency
- **JavaScript**: Interactive elements, smooth scrolling, animations
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with breakpoints at 968px and 640px
- **Performance**: Fade-in animations, parallax effects, scroll progress indicator

#### Brand Identity
- **Color Palette**: Coral-red (#E85D55), accent pink (#FF8B85), clean whites
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (headings), Inter (body)
- **Visual Style**: Premium, clean, modern with cultural authenticity

### 4. Conversion Optimization Strategy

#### Current Conversion Points
✓ Hero CTAs (Menu exploration + Location finding)
✓ Menu section CTA (Visit Us Today)
✓ Visit section CTAs (Call Now + Get Directions)

#### Recommended Enhancements for Higher Conversion

**Priority 1: Critical (High Impact, Easy Implementation)**

1. **Add Social Proof Elements**
   - [ ] Customer testimonials section with photos
   - [ ] Google Reviews integration or rating display
   - [ ] Instagram feed or photo gallery of real customers
   - [ ] Press mentions or awards (if applicable)
   - **Impact:** Trust-building increases conversion by 20-40%

2. **Strengthen First-Time Visitor Offers**
   - [ ] Limited-time welcome offer (e.g., "First-time visitors: Free HK Milk Tea with any meal")
   - [ ] Display promotion in hero section or sticky banner
   - [ ] Create urgency without being aggressive
   - **Impact:** Incentivizes immediate action, reduces decision paralysis

3. **Add Real Photography**
   - [ ] Replace all image placeholders with professional photos
   - [ ] Hero background: Cafe interior or signature dish
   - [ ] Menu items: High-quality food photography
   - [ ] Story section: Chef/team photos or cafe ambiance
   - **Impact:** Visual appeal drives 60% more engagement

4. **Implement Click-to-Call & Click-to-Navigate**
   - [ ] Ensure phone number is clickable on mobile (tel: link)
   - [ ] Add Google Maps embed or direct navigation link
   - [ ] Add "Book a Table" CTA if reservations are accepted
   - **Impact:** Removes friction, increases immediate action by 30%

**Priority 2: Important (High Impact, Moderate Effort)**

5. **Add Scarcity/Urgency Elements**
   - [ ] Display current wait times or "Now serving" indicator
   - [ ] Show daily specials or limited-availability items
   - [ ] Highlight "Fresh baked daily - while supplies last" for popular items
   - **Impact:** Creates FOMO (fear of missing out), drives urgency

6. **Create Interactive Menu Experience**
   - [ ] Hoverable menu items that expand to show more details
   - [ ] Filter by meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Drinks)
   - [ ] Dietary indicators (Vegetarian, Gluten-free, etc.)
   - [ ] Price transparency (if appropriate for brand positioning)
   - **Impact:** Reduces uncertainty, increases confidence in visiting

7. **Add "What to Expect" Section**
   - [ ] Typical visit duration
   - [ ] Seating capacity and wait times
   - [ ] Parking information
   - [ ] Dress code (casual, family-friendly, etc.)
   - **Impact:** Removes anxiety for first-time visitors

8. **Email Capture for Future Marketing**
   - [ ] "Join our mailing list for weekly specials" opt-in
   - [ ] Place subtly in footer or as exit-intent popup
   - [ ] Offer incentive (10% off next visit, free drink, etc.)
   - **Impact:** Builds remarketing audience, repeat customer potential

**Priority 3: Nice-to-Have (Medium Impact, Higher Effort)**

9. **Video Content Integration**
   - [ ] Short 30-second brand video in hero or story section
   - [ ] Behind-the-scenes of milk tea preparation
   - [ ] Chef introduction or cooking demonstration
   - **Impact:** Increases engagement time, builds emotional connection

10. **Blog/Content Section (Future Phase)**
    - [ ] Stories about Hong Kong culture
    - [ ] Recipe deep-dives and cooking techniques
    - [ ] Customer spotlights and community features
    - **Impact:** SEO benefits, positions as cultural authority

11. **Multi-language Support**
    - [ ] Traditional Chinese translation toggle
    - [ ] Cantonese audio pronunciation guide for menu items
    - **Impact:** Serves Hong Kong expat community better, shows authenticity

12. **Online Ordering Integration** (Consider carefully for authenticity)
    - [ ] Only if aligned with brand - Cha Chaan Tengs are about in-person experience
    - [ ] Could offer "Skip the line" pre-ordering for pickup
    - **Impact:** Convenience vs. brand authenticity trade-off

### 5. Conversion Funnel Optimization

#### Current Customer Journey
1. **Discovery** → Search/Social Media/Word of Mouth
2. **Landing** → Hero Section (Value proposition)
3. **Education** → Heritage Section (Cultural context)
4. **Interest** → Menu Section (Product showcase)
5. **Conviction** → Story Section (Trust and authenticity)
6. **Action** → Visit Section (Conversion point)

#### Recommended Journey Enhancements

**Stage 1: Landing (Hero)**
- Add trust indicator (e.g., "Featured in [Publication]" or "5-star rated")
- Include enticing visual (food photo or cafe atmosphere)
- Clarify unique selling proposition more boldly

**Stage 2: Engagement (Heritage)**
- Keep as-is (strong educational content)
- Consider adding 1-2 customer quotes about authenticity

**Stage 3: Desire (Menu)**
- Add prices (transparent) OR keep hidden (premium positioning) - test both
- Include "Chef's Recommendations" badge for decision-paralyzed visitors
- Add allergy/dietary information

**Stage 4: Trust (Story + Social Proof)**
- Add new section: Customer testimonials with photos
- Include media mentions or awards
- Show "Real customers enjoying our food" Instagram gallery

**Stage 5: Action (Visit)**
- Strengthen urgency: "Visit us today - freshly baked items sell out by 2pm"
- Add map with parking instructions
- Include "What our regulars order" suggestions
