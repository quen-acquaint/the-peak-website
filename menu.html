A Cha Chaan Teng in Glebe
Hong Kong, in every plate.
香 港 茶 餐 廳
Born in 1950s Hong Kong, the cha chaan teng is the art of fast, fortifying food made with care. Here in Glebe, we keep the formula honest: thick scrambled eggs over toast, milk tea pulled through a stocking, baked rice under the salamander. Old recipes, made each morning.
